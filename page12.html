Other Forms

(2025, synthesized soundscapes, sample manipulation, live instrumentation, manipulated video projections, premiering at Custer Institute and Observatory, Peconic, NY)

Other Forms is an otherworldly, multi-dimensional soundscape inspired by contrasts and similarities, and the places where the two intersect. Starting with the music itself – analog and digital, light and dark, quiet and intense – this exploration of contrasts expands into original projection art featuring the stark, abstract photography of Charles Nickles paired with manipulated found footage of the natural and human worlds unfolding in ordinary and extraordinary ways.
