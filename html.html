Infinite Exhale

(forthcoming, expanding video loop, meditators)

Demo of full video.

There’s so much we’re holding in. All against an endless loop of bad news, social feed doom scrolls, and an impending sense of dread. As a response, Infinite Exhale is an endless release.

For Infinite Exhale, people were invited to sit, take a deep breath, visualize something they wanted to let out, and… exhale. The project plans to endlessly expand, creating a piece that can always be added to.
